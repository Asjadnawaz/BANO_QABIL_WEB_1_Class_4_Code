Integrations

Connect AgentForge with the LLM providers, developer tools, and data stores you already use.


Bring Your Own Model

AgentForge is model-agnostic. Connect to any major LLM provider and switch between models without changing your agent logic.

OpenAI

Full support for GPT-4o, GPT-4 Turbo, and o1 models. Use function calling, structured outputs, and streaming responses out of the box.


Anthropic

Native Claude integration with tool use, extended thinking, and 200K context windows. Ideal for complex reasoning and code generation agents


Google Gemini

Connect to Gemini 2.0 and Gemini Pro models via Vertex AI or the Gemini API. Multimodal support for text, image, and video inputs.
