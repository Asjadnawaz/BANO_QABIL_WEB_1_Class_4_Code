About AgentForge

We're on a mission to make autonomous AI agents accessible to every developer. Learn about our story, values, and team.


Making AI Agents Accessible to Every Developer

“To democratize autonomous AI by giving every developer the tools to build, deploy, and scale intelligent agents — without needing a PhD in machine learning.”

Founded in 2023, AgentForge was born from the frustration of building production AI agents. Our founders — veterans of Google Brain, OpenAI, and Amazon — saw teams spending months on agent infrastructure instead of building the actual intelligence. We set out to change that. Today, AgentForge powers over 100,000 AI agents across startups and Fortune 500 companies. Our platform handles the hard parts — orchestration, memory, tool integration, monitoring — so developers can focus on what makes their agents unique.
